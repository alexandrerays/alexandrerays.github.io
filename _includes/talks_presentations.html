  • How to create an object detection project from scratch to deploy?

  • Machine Learning Canvas: From data collection to value proposition
    Description: Systems based on Machine Learning are complex. It is common to observe that some models do not solve the problems they should solve and end up not being used in practice.   • How to make versioning control of data and machine learning models using DVC?
    Description: Have you created so many datasets and so many models that you no longer know who is who? Don't remember which version of the model is in production anymore? Another Data Scientist joined the project and she/he can't reproduce the experiments? In this talk, we'll explore how Open Source Data Version Control (DVC) can help solve these problems.   • Trends in the use of artificial intelligence in autonomous vehicles
    Description: Safety-critical systems have the potential to produce catastrophic consequences for society, such as deaths, injuries, environmental and material damage. In this way, developing and ensuring that critical systems are safe throughout their life cycle is a mission of engineering, science and technology. However, new technologies and paradigms have made security assurance approaches for critical systems obsolete. Among them, the massive and pervasive use of advanced levels of Artificial Intelligence can be highlighted. This lecture will address the state of the art of the application of Artificial Intelligence in safety-critical systems, with emphasis on autonomous vehicles. July 2018, The Developer's Conference, São Paulo - SP, Brazil. [slides]

  • Fintechs: technology and inovation in financial services
    Description: The digital age and connectivity have profoundly transformed the way we interact with the world around us. More and more, we need to be aware of the latest technologies so that we can have some kind of advantage, whether to save time, increase productivity or even to save money. In the financial market, Fintechs develop technological innovations aimed at this sector. They attack serious problems that we have in the Brazilian financial system, such as high interest rates on loans, bureaucratization and lack of transparency. This lecture will address the general context of Fintechs and bring a vision of how they are inserted in our daily lives. July 2017, The Developer's Conference, São Paulo - SP, Brazil. [slides]
